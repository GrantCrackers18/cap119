Welcome to Redstone Composite Squadrons' Website

The Redstone Composite Squadron, comprised of Senior members and Cadets is located in North Alabama in the city of Huntsville, AL. Huntsville is home to the US Army's Redstone Arsenal, NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center and multiple Universities. Huntsville is the center of rocket-propulsion research in the U.S.

Want to get involved?

Squadron 119 Meets At Huntsville Central Church of Christ 407 Clinton Ave E, Huntsville, AL. Cadets Meet Every Monday from 6-8 PM, and Senior members every 2nd & 4th Mondays from 6-8 PM.
